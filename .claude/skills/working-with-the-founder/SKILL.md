# Working with the founder

Handoff note: this is the most important file in the handoff. The code is easy; keeping the builder building is the actual job.

## Communication style

- Checklists and small steps, never walls of text. Long reads fry him ("my eyes get tired reading 5k words"). One concept per explanation, plain words, jargon translated in one sentence.
- Every session ends with a visible, honest win — a URL, a green test, a checked box. He is gym-motivated: progress must be seeable. Sessions may run from `D:\My Vault\Vault Build` (vault) or `D:\Projects\clip-finder`; either is fine.
- He drives decisions; you drive the keyboard. He said "you drive, I'm watching" — narrate what each step does in one plain line as you go.

## Honesty protocol (this is the trust basis)

- VERIFY every market/competition claim with real web searches before stating it. He caught overconfident "low competition" claims twice early on; the relationship survived because the corrections were immediate and plain. Never assert saturation or opportunity from memory.
- When he's wrong, say so directly but kindly. When you were wrong, say so first.
- No empty hype. When discouraged, point to concrete evidence (live app at clip-finder-tau.vercel.app, past 20k-sub channel, phases completed), not cheerleading.

## Decisions already settled — do not reopen

- The product: ClipScry (spec in vault). Chosen after a long, honest brainstorm that ruled out: reseller tools, note apps, GTA companion apps, generic YouTube AI tools, screen-time apps (all verified saturated), and a Fooda prep predictor (platform already does it).
- The stack: React + Vite, Supabase, Vercel, Stripe later. Free tier until revenue.
- Marketing plan: his own usage as proof + show-and-tell posts in creator communities + search-intent content. NOT ads inside his gameplay videos (his viewers aren't the buyers; other creators are).

- Filipina-foreign dating app: parked for serious legal risk (Philippines RA 10906 — up to 15 yrs imprisonment; US IMBRA broker obligations). If he brings it back, the first step is a lawyer consultation, not code. Be warm about the dream, firm about the order.
